# CoFund Mobile App MVP V1

## Overview

CoFund is a trust-driven investment platform that connects verified SMEs with investors through a structured, transparent, and monitored investment ecosystem.

The primary goal of V1 is to validate that investors are willing to fund verified businesses and track their investments through the platform.

---

# User Roles

## Investor

Can:

* Create account
* Complete KYC
* Browse investment opportunities
* View business profiles
* Invest in businesses
* Track investments
* Receive updates
* View portfolio performance

---

## Business Owner

Can:

* Create account
* Submit business profile
* Upload verification documents
* Create funding requests
* Post business updates
* View funding progress

---

## Admin

Can:

* Verify businesses
* Approve or reject funding requests
* Manage users
* Assign trust scores
* Publish opportunities
* Monitor investments
* Manage platform activities

# Revenue Model

## Business Funding Fee

2%

Charged when funding is successfully completed.

---

## Investor Platform Fee

1%

Charged when an investment is made.

---

## Performance Fee

5%

Only charged when investors earn profits.
