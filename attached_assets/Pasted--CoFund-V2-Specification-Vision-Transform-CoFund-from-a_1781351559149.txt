# CoFund V2 Specification

## Vision

Transform CoFund from a simple SME funding platform into a complete private investment ecosystem for Africa.

V2 introduces liquidity, premium memberships, networking, advanced analytics, and additional investment models.

---

# New Modules

## 1. CoFund Pro

Premium subscription for investors.

### Benefits

* Early access to new opportunities
* Advanced investment analytics
* Portfolio performance reports
* Higher withdrawal limits
* Priority support
* Exclusive investment opportunities
* Investor badges

### Subscription Plans

* Monthly
* Quarterly
* Annual

---

## 2. Secondary Market

Allow investors to sell investments before maturity.

### Features

* List investment for sale
* Buy investment from another investor
* Transfer ownership
* Transaction history
* Marketplace filtering

### Revenue

* Seller fee
* Buyer fee
* Transfer fee

---

## 3. Wallet System

### Features

* Deposit funds
* Withdraw funds
* Internal wallet transfers
* Transaction history
* Earnings wallet
* Pending returns wallet

---

## 4. Investor Verification

### KYC Levels

#### Tier 1

* Phone verification
* Email verification

#### Tier 2

* Government ID
* Selfie verification

#### Tier 3

* Proof of address
* Bank verification

Benefits increase with verification level.

---

## 5. Business Trust Center

Expanded verification system.

### Trust Components

* Business Verification
* CAC Verification
* Financial Verification
* Operational Verification
* Site Visit Verification
* Team Verification

Generate dynamic Trust Scores.

---

## 6. Business Analytics Dashboard

Business owners can view:

* Investor growth
* Funding trends
* Funding completion rates
* Engagement metrics
* Project performance

---

## 7. Investor Analytics Dashboard

Display:

* Portfolio value
* Profit earned
* ROI percentage
* Sector exposure

* Historical growth chart

## 8. Business Networking Hub

Businesses can discover:

* Investors
* Strategic partners
* Suppliers
* Joint venture partners
* Service providers

Like LinkedIn for SMEs.

## 9. Investor-Business Messaging

### Features

* Direct messaging
* Project discussions
* Due diligence questions
* Investor support tickets

---

## 10. Featured Business Placement

Businesses can pay for visibility.

### Placement Types

* Homepage Featured
* Trending Opportunities
* Sponsored Listing

Revenue source for CoFund.

---

## 11. Real Estate Investments

Allow property-based opportunities.

### Models

* Fractional ownership
* Rental income sharing
* Property development projects

Investor dashboard tracks:

* Rental income
* Property value
* Capital appreciation

---

## 12. Equipment Leasing

Businesses request equipment.

Example:

* Truck
* Generator
* Manufacturing machine
* Agricultural equipment

Investors fund equipment.

CoFund purchases and leases equipment to businesses.

---

## 13. Referral System

### Investors

Earn rewards for inviting investors.

### Businesses

Earn rewards for introducing SMEs.

---

## 14. Ratings & Reviews

Investors can review:

* Businesses
* Opportunities
* Communication quality

Businesses can review:

* Investors
* Partners

---

## 15. Advanced Notifications

Push notifications for:

* Funding milestones
* New opportunities
* Returns paid
* Investment maturity
* Market opportunities
